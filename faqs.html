Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Does waxing hurt? ▲
Everyone has different pain tolerance, what may hurt for you may not hurt for other people. However, our wax is made to hurt a lot less for all types of people.
Why don't you offer eyebrow waxing as a service? ▲
The skin around your eyebrows tend to be a lot thinner and more sensitive, Waxing the brow bone could cause premature sagging with too much tugging of the skin.
How long is my appointment considered valid? ▲
All bookings are considered active unless you cancel it within the prescribed period. But heads up! Your account may be disabled if you accumulate an exceeding amount of cancellations, rescheduled, or no-shows!
